I'm a Browser Artist, residing in Ladysmith, BC, Canada. I work with a team I love, at Domain7. I have a wife and three children.

I've been honing my craft for 8 years. For the first 5 I operated my design / development company, Vertical Grain Design, working with my own clients and also contracting for agencies such as Rethink, Dossier Creative, Also Known As, and Briteweb. For the last 3 years I've had the pleasure of working with the good folk at Domain7.

I'm a markup / styling nerd, and am not satisfied with less than perfect markup accross all devices. I'm into the latest styling tools such as LESS, SCSS, PostCSS, and BEM methodology.

I've worked with a number of CMS. My level of expertise is highest in Wordpress, where I create custom CMS for clients. Lately I'm excited about the Wordpress JSON api. I'm also fluent with Expression Engine and Craft, and have dabbled in Drupal and Rails. I get really excited about ultra lean javascript CMS ideas, from git based FoxFlow, to Firebase single page apps, all the way to a google spreadsheet backend paired with a jquery frontend.

I like Javascript. jQuery, of course, also Node development, ES6, Firebase, front-end frameworks like React and Angular, and Git based FoxFlow.

I really enjoy making weird things like Iapetus, Oscar, truefacts.

I like cooking and I made a lean, mobile friendly recipe site, MixMeals.com, to keep all my favourite meals
